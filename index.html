More Details

Join us for a lush and leafy adventure at our Plant Picnic, where green thumbs and foliage enthusiasts come together for a day of plant magic.

Not only will you enjoy a delightful picnic in the heart of nature, but we're adding a twist - a Plant Swap! Bring your beloved plant friends, and trade them for new leafy companions.

It's an opportunity to expand your collection and make new plant-loving friends. Let's gather, grow, and swap stories and plants!

Don't forget to bring some food & drinks for yourself!

Plant Swap Rules

The rules are simple: kindly ask if someone would like to swap, and it's perfectly okay if they politely decline.

Remember, we have newcomers who are just discovering the joy of plants, so let's be welcoming and considerate.

If one person isn't interested, feel free to move on to the next potential plant buddy.

Happy swapping and sharing the plant love!
